Snake Game (CSC Final Project)

you want the game starts. You must start by moving right. You are not allowed to move the opposite direction you are going, for example if you are moving
right you cannot go left, and if you are going up you canâ€™t immediately go down. You get points by getting the food under the direct center of the snake. The goal
